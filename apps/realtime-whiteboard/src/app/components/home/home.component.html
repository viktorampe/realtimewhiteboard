Setup session

Start a realtime whiteboard session by clicking the 'Start session' button in the navigation panel.campus-carddetailitem. Choose a name and a pincode for your session.

Start session

Click 'Create' after you filled in the form to start the session. The whiteboard will lauch shortly after you completed the setup. You are almost ready to start playing!

Play!

You can start playing now! Let other people join your session by sharing the link. You can track who manipulated wich card by clicking the 'Card Info' button.
